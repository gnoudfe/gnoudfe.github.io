About The Formation And Development.

Establish in 1959

Hanoi University having passed the years of inception and continued development. During those 60 years the School has had a number of name changes Each of these events is associated with ups and downs and historical and social contexts in each period such as: School of Foreign Languages (1959-1960), Foreign Language Supplement School (1960-1967), University of Foreign Languages ​​(1967-1978), College of Foreign Language Supplementation (1978-1984), University of Foreign Languages ​​(1984-2006) ) and from 2006 to present is Hanoi University.

The stage ahead is full of difficulties, but the entire team of Hanoi University today, with relatively young age and full of enthusiasm, is ready to continue the proud tradition and develop the University. global integration, striving to become an applied university present in the high ranking map of universities in Asia.

International Relation

One of the important contents for the synchronous, creative and effective implementation of foreign affairs activities identified by the 13th National Congress of the Party is "Building a comprehensive and modern diplomacy with three pillars: Party diplomacy. , state diplomacy and people's diplomacy”. This is both a content that inherits the spirit of the previous congresses and is a new development in awareness about the content, roles, and ways of coordinating and deploying foreign activities. To effectively implement this content in practice, it is necessary to clarify the perception of party diplomacy, state diplomacy and people's diplomacy, as well as the relationship between these three pillars in the new situation.
